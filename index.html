Project portfolio

Business Analysis · Systems Analysis · George Brown College
LMS Course Management Improvement Initiative
Brightspace LMS · BABoK-Guided Elicitation · Use Case Engineering

This project addressed a real operational problem at George Brown College: multi-day delays caused by the absence of an automated cross-listing process in the Brightspace LMS. Working with a real program coordinator and college instructor, I conducted structured BABoK-guided elicitation sessions, mapped current and future state processes, and delivered a comprehensive set of BA artifacts spanning 13 epics across 4 functional domains.

The full use case specification for cross-listing management includes alternative flows, business rules, a state diagram, and an element data dictionary. The work identified specific manual bottlenecks causing multi-day delays and defined the future state solution in terms an engineering team could act on directly.
